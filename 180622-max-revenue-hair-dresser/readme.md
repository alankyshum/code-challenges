> An array of charges for hair dressing is given, and the hair dresser cannot take 2 jobs consecutively
>
> (e.g.) Can take job 0 and job 2, but not job 0 -> job 1
>
> i.e. 30 -> 15, or 30 -> 90, but not 30 -> 45
